# Peace-Driven Leader™ Activation Pathway

## 🌿 Core Philosophy
A guided, calming onboarding journey designed to help leaders "offload their cares." The experience aims to make the user feel seen, held, and stabilized before handing back their responsibilities in an organized, peaceful fashion.

---

## ✨ Phase 0: Initiation (Celebration)
**Moment**: Immediately after payment.
- **Experience**: Transition from payment success to a "Welcome Home" celebration moment.
- **Messaging**: "You no longer have to carry everything alone."
- **Email**: Includes personal GIFs and a conversational voice-note style message.
- **CTA**: "Enter Your Welcome Suite" → Redirect to Phase 1.

---

## 🖥️ Phase 1: Connection (The Welcome Suite)
**Objective**: "I feel seen and welcomed."

### Step 1A: Welcome Video
- **Focus**: Relatable, human-first introduction.
- **Tone**: Simplicity over overwhelm. "We’re not rushing. We’re aligning."

### Step 1B: Getting to Know You (Quick Snapshot)
- **Time**: 5–7 minutes.
- **Focus**: Insight for personalization without "deep digging."

### Step 1C: Leadership Triage (Mind, Body, Spirit)
**Mind (The Wiring)**:
- [High Functioning Neurodiversity Assessment](https://exceptionalindividuals.com/neurodiversity/)
- Internal Human Design / CliftonStrengths
- [PDL Leader Score Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1iYCURCTSHcaqVVYYyfa_iz9RkNsF1FRRFoTnMoCMWxE/edit?usp=sharing)

**Body (The Interaction)**:
- [Free DiSC Test](https://discpersonalitytesting.com/free-disc-test/) (Physical interaction style)

**Spirit (The Divine Identity)**:
- [Spiritual Gifts Assessment](https://giftstest.com/)
- 1:1 Call with Client Success Guide.

### Step 1D: Open Share (Emotional Safety)
- **Prompt**: "Is there anything on your heart, mind, or plate that you want me to be aware of?"
- **Format**: Large text comment box.

### Step 1E: Getting to Know Us (Culture Layer)
- Vision / Mission / Culture Videos.
- PDL "Lingo" PDF Reflection Guide & Key Terms video.

### Step 1F: Book Your Call
- **Strategy**: Focus on daily progress and getting tasks done before the live onboarding call.

---

## 💠 Phase 2: Awareness (Rhythm & Momentum)
**Objective**: "I see my life clearly now."

### 360° Leadership Evaluation (Observation Experiment)
- [Observation Doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/16tNLOz4_mAbdwWcCAo48_qB0ARVBspluzDVh4tCxZyU/edit?usp=drivesdk)
- **Action**: User sends a curated script to 1 partner, 1-2 children, and 1 trusted person.
- **Goal**: Understand how leadership is experienced by others.

### Additional Awareness Inputs
1. **Rhythm Snapshot**: [5 Discovery Questions](https://docs.google.com/document/d/16P2RQ-SeNvvUD-mX_UqBQLItpJ_5GMKP08tBkM60sIM/edit?usp=sharing).
2. **B.O.S.S Index**: Boundaries, Organization, Sustainable Systems.
3. **Evening Check**: Text-based walkthrough of evening routine.
4. **Capacity Pulse**: [Roles/Responsibilities Checklist](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ROXoWkKDm7tnb0pLnMfFYFIkx9s8j64JKkVL6sDrtqw/edit?usp=sharing).

### Micro Commitment Tracker
- Gamified checkboxes: "I've sent my 360° Observation," etc.

---

## 🌿 Phase 3: Stabilization (Embody the Vision)
**Objective**: "I am stepping into the life I desire."

- **3A: Vision Activation**: Write descriptions for 8 life domains.
- **3B: Vision Statements**: "I am thankful that I..." (Aligning with outcomes).
- **3C: Ideal Day Narrative**: Written walkthrough from waking up to sleeping.
- **3D: Word of the Year**: One word to anchor leadership and life.
- **3E: Family Mission Statement**: 3–4 Core Values + 1 Unified Mission Statement.

---

## 🔥 Phase 4: Realignment & Kickoff
**Objective**: "I am fully activated and supported."

- **4A: Book Kickstart Call**: Rules of the game, rewards, and milestones.
- **4B: Join Telegram**: Group-based support.
- **4C: Book Wealth Strategist Call**: Financial peace (Cash + Credit alignment).

---

## 🛠️ Technical Focus
1. **Database**: All text inputs (Open Share, Evening Check, Vision Statements) must be persisted to the MongoDB `User` model.
2. **UI/UX**: Minimalist, calm design. No voice/image features for the initial MVP.
3. **Progress**: Sidebar must reflect this updated 4-phase journey with granular sub-steps.
